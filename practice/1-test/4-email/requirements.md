Email address structure: local-part@domain.ext
- local-part and domain can contain letters, numbers, dots (not adjacent) and dashes (-)
- domain should contain extension (for instance, .com)
Valid examples:
JohnDoe@gmail.com
some-email@mail.ru
some.email4@mail.ru
Invalid examples:
some%email@gmail.com
some@gmail
some..email@mail.ru
